How to Get a Loan Serial Enquiry Report

  1. Log in to Pronto.
  2. Click Loans and Repairs → Serial Enquiry.
  3. At the top, select your warehouse (Z + Store Name).
  4. Export the report:
    • Installed: Right-click tab → Export Data.
    • Web: Settings (cog) → Export Data.
